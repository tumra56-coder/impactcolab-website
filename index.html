What We Do

Decoding complexity
into clarity.

We work with any organisation with a vision to achieve meaningful change in the world. We help you gain clarity on the change you want to see and how you hope to get there — and equip you with powerful, AI-powered tools that will help you, as a team and as an organisation, grow your understanding of how change happens, evidence it, and bring that journey of change to life.

Our philosophy is simple: it's your intelligence that matters. Your collective wisdom, your frontline knowledge, your team's shared understanding of how change happens. Decode Agent, our trained AI evaluation assistant, takes that intelligence and analyses it against our bespoke frameworks to surface clarity, sharpen your thinking, and build a dynamic impact and evidence system your whole team owns.

You can work with us directly — through facilitation, evaluation design, and strategic support. Or use The CoLab independently, guided by Decode Agent, at your own pace. Either way, the intelligence at the centre is always yours.

What I Offer

Facilitation, evaluation and AI-powered training and tools that help organisations direct, evidence and understand the change they are making in the world.

Facilitation & Strategy

Working with teams to surface collective wisdom, build shared understanding, and map a clear pathway to impact. Whether you are starting from scratch or revisiting your strategy, we bring structured facilitation, training and deep evaluation expertise to help you think clearly about the change you want to make and how to get there.

Monitoring & Learning Systems

Building practical, lightweight systems that connect your impact pathway directly to how you collect evidence — so measurement is meaningful, not just mandatory. We design frameworks your team will actually use, that generate insight rather than just data.

Evaluation

Designing and leading evaluations that go beyond compliance to surface what is genuinely working, what isn't, and why. Our approach is participatory — grounded in the knowledge of the people closest to the work — and our outputs are designed to drive decisions, not just document them.

The CoLab — Digital Tool

Our AI-powered platform guides your team through the full pathway — from collective thinking to evaluation framework, data collection system, and report. Available alongside facilitation services or independently. We also offer hands-on training sessions so your team can run The CoLab with confidence and embed it into your practice.

Enter the CoLab

The CoLab brings your team together to explore the complexity of your work and your environment — combining collective wisdom, lived experience, and diverse perspectives into a shared understanding of how change happens. Guided by Decode Agent, your team's thinking is synthesised into a clear, structured pathway to impact that your whole organisation owns.

That pathway connects directly to how you collect evidence — automatically and without extra work. Teams log observations, record stories of change, and tag evidence to specific outcomes. The CoLab works whether your team is in a room together or contributing remotely, with a facilitator or independently. The result is a living theory of change, grounded in your own thinking, that grows as your work does.

Meet Decode Agent

Decode Agent: Your AI evaluation assistant.

Trained to think like an evaluator. Built to amplify what your team already knows.

I'm Decode Agent — the AI at the heart of The CoLab. I've been trained specifically in evaluation methodology, theory of change, and participatory approaches. I know what good evidence looks like. I know how to ask the right questions. And I'm genuinely quite good at turning messy, complicated thinking into something clear and useful.

But here's the thing — without your team's collective intelligence, I'm just a very sophisticated blank page. Every insight, every outcome, every story of change that comes out of The CoLab starts with you. I just help decode it.

I synthesise your responses into impact pathways, surface assumptions you might not have noticed, ask the questions your team hasn't thought to ask, and embed feedback loops so your organisation keeps learning as the work evolves.

Who I Am

I am an evaluator and facilitator based in Malawi, deeply embedded in the international development sector. My work is grounded in the belief that evaluation shouldn't just be about reporting to donors — it should be a powerful tool for organisational learning and adaptation.

Over the years I have supported numerous organisations to measure what actually matters, surface genuine insights, and strengthen their practice from the ground up. I work with NGOs, charities, social enterprises and independent practitioners — particularly those doing complex work in resource-constrained environments.

decodeCoLab grew directly from this practice. The platform exists because of structured thinking, clear frameworks, and AI as a collaborative tool — not because of technical expertise. That process is itself a demonstration of the methodology.
