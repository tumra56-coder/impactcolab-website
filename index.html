Who I Am

I am an evaluator and facilitator based in Malawi, deeply embedded in the international development sector. My work is grounded in the belief that evaluation shouldn't just be about reporting to donors — it should be a powerful tool for organisational learning and adaptation.

Over the years, I have supported numerous organisations to measure what actually matters, surface genuine insights, and strengthen their practice from the ground up.

I am the founder of Decode:CoLab, a digital platform born from these experiences — currently being tested with local organisations across Malawi to make participatory evaluation accessible and actionable.
